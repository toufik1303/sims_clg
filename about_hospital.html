Hospital overview

The SIMS will provide would provide top quality clinical services in all specialties. It is well equipped with a state of the art Intensive Care Unit (ICU) and 5 large Operation Theatres. Eminent Doctors & Nurses drawn from all over the country have been recruited full time. ETHICS with transparency of operations will be one of our distinguishing features.


The hospital would be catering to the population in a radius between 5 to 10 km from SIMS, in addition to the staff and students on campus. We plan to set up telemedicine services in all the adjoining villages to facilitate providing expert medical advice from our consultants. This will ensure outreach services to peripheral, far flung and access compromised settlements.

Our Multispecialty services include Opthalmology (Eye), Endocrinology (Glands), Dialysis and Nephrology (Kidney), Cardiology (Heart) and Podiatry (Foot). With our expanded services – Bariatric Surgery (for weight loss), Gastroenterology, General surgery, Orthopedics, ENT and Gynecology Services, SIMS Hospital now provides comprehensive and end-to- end patient care under one roof.

Dr. Vedprakash Mishra
 Chief Advisor

Hon’ble Dr. Vedprakash Mishra who is presently is the National Head of the Academic Programme of Indian Programme UNESCO Chair in Bio-Ethics Haifa, is an illustrious member of the medical fraternity, well known not only in India but also overseas. A dynamic orator, he is a keen academician and an astute administrator. Born in Nagpur and educated with a MD degree, he streamlined his life-path to equip himself by completing Master of Industrial Relation and Personnel Management and Bachelor’s in Journalism as well. He has been conferred with the highest academic distinction of D. conferred with D.Sc. (Honoris Cause) by Six Health Sciences Universities in the country in recognition of his selfless services to the cause of medical education of consequence and relevance at the National and International level.

His association with the Medical Council of India in various capacities has been of well over three decades whereat he held prestigious position that of Chairman of the Postgraduate Medical Education Committee, Chairman of the Finance Committee, Member of the Executive Committee, Chairman of the Academic Committee and several others.
